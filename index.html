Gameplay

Three things stand between you and defeat

01

Build Your Maze

Place towers freely on the battlefield. Every tower also acts as a wall. shape the enemy's path into a lethal gauntlet. No two mazes are ever the same.

02

Play Your Cards

A deck of spell cards gives you tactical options every wave. rain fire on the path, buff your towers, slow enemies, or generate gold. Spend mana wisely.

03

Defend to the End

Enemies pour in wave after wave. If they reach the castle, you lose HP. Run out and the keep falls. Solo, co-op, or PvP. the castle is always at stake.

Features

Built for depth, not complexity

10 Towers

Arrow, Cannon, Freeze, Sniper, Bomb, Tesla, Poison, Ballista, Mage, Watch Tower. each with a distinct role.

30 Spell Cards

Attack, buff, trap, economy, draw. seven card categories across four rarity tiers.

Dual Deck System

A permanent tower build deck and a consumable spell hand. Two systems, one strategy.

15+ Enemy Types

From nimble Thieves and armoured Trolls to the elusive Gargoyle that ignores your maze entirely.

Maze Pathfinding

Live pathfinding. enemies always find the longest safe route through your tower layout.

3 Maps at Launch

Each map with a unique tileset, layout, and visual theme to change how you build.
